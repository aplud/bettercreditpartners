2. Page-by-Page Outline (With Compliance Notes)
1️⃣ Home Page

Goal: Explain value without promising results or timelines.

Sections

Hero Section:

Headline (safe language):

“Professional Credit Education & Dispute Assistance”

Sub-text:

“We help clients understand their credit reports and assist with disputing inaccurate, unverifiable, or outdated information.”

What We Do (3 columns):

Credit Report Review

Dispute Assistance

Credit Education & Strategy

How We’re Different:

No guarantees

CROA-compliant

Transparent process

Client education focused

⚠️ Avoid on homepage:

“Guaranteed results”

“Boost your score in 30 days”

Specific point increases

2️⃣ How It Works

This page protects you legally.

Step-by-Step

Free Credit Evaluation

Soft pull or client-provided reports only

Personalized Action Plan

Based on FCRA-allowed disputes

Dispute Assistance

We help draft and submit disputes

Ongoing Education & Monitoring Guidance

Compliance Footer Callout

“Results vary. We do not remove accurate, current, or verifiable information.”

3️⃣ Services Page

Break services into educational + assistance, not “repair promises.”

Examples

Credit Report Analysis

Dispute Letter Preparation

Creditor Communication Assistance

Credit Building Education

Identity Issue Guidance (if applicable)

⚠️ Avoid

“Debt elimination”

“We remove collections”

“We force bureaus to delete”

4️⃣ Pricing Page

This is where Florida + Texas rules matter most.

Structure

Monthly plan (recommended vs upfront)

Clear statement:

“You are not charged for services that have not yet been performed.”

Mandatory Disclosure Block

“Federal law prohibits credit repair companies from charging fees before services are fully performed unless permitted by state law and properly bonded.”

For Florida

State that advance payments are collected only because bonding/trust requirements are met (if true).

For Texas

Recommend post-service billing only unless you are fully registered and bonded.

5️⃣ About Us

Humanizes the company, reduces regulatory scrutiny.

Include

Mission statement

Education-first philosophy

Compliance commitment

States served (explicitly list FL & TX)

6️⃣ Contact Page

Required

Physical mailing address (or registered agent address)

Email

Phone number

Business hours

⚠️ Texas strongly prefers transparency here.

7️⃣ Legal & Disclosures Page (CRITICAL)

This should be its own page, not buried.

Federal CROA Disclosure

Include:

Right to cancel within 3 business days

No guarantee of results

No misleading claims

Client can dispute directly with bureaus

Florida Disclosure

Example language:

“Better Credit Consulting complies with Florida’s Credit Service Organizations Act and maintains a $10,000 surety bond or trust account as required by law.”

Texas Disclosure

Example language:

“Better Credit Consulting is registered as a Credit Services Organization with the Texas Secretary of State and maintains the required surety bond pursuant to Texas Finance Code Chapter 393.”

8️⃣ Privacy Policy

Data handling

Credit report security

No resale of personal data

9️⃣ Terms of Service

Service limitations

No legal advice disclaimer

Arbitration clause (optional but common)

3. State-Specific Implementation Notes (Important)
Florida

✔ Bond or trust account must exist before advance payments
✔ Website must NOT imply licensing if not required
✔ Clear cancellation + refund language

Texas

✔ Registration before offering services
✔ Bond must be active and referenced accurately
✔ Annual renewal reminder (internal compliance, not public)
